History of Paris

Paris, the capital of France, has a history that stretches back to ancient times. Known as the "City of Light," Paris has been a global center of art, fashion, and culture for centuries. From the medieval era to the French Revolution and beyond, Paris has played a crucial role in shaping world history.

Must-Visit Locations in Paris

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most famous landmarks in the world. Standing at 324 meters, it offers stunning views of Paris.

Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of other works of art, the Louvre is the world's largest and most-visited art museum.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame is a masterpiece of French Gothic architecture. Despite the 2019 fire, it's still a symbol of Parisian history.
